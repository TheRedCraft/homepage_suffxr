Why Nebula?

Nebula is a versatile Discord bot designed to elevate your server's functionality and make your Discord experience more enjoyable. With a wide range of commands at your disposal, Nebula is your go-to bot for everything from moderation to giveaways and more.

Add To Server Support Server

Moderation

Nebula's moderation features are designed to help keep your server safe and under control. Whether you need to kick or ban a member, mute someone for a period of time, or simply manage your server's channels and permissions, Nebula has you covered.

Tickets

Nebula's ticket system allows users to create support tickets for assistance. Users can easily submit their issues or questions, and your staff members can efficiently manage and respond to them, ensuring a smooth support experience for your server.

Message Levels

With Nebula's message levels feature, you can set up different message levels for roles within your server. This allows users to progress and unlock additional features or privileges based on their activity and engagement in the community. Keep your members motivated and reward them for their contributions.

Welcome Messages

With Nebula's welcome message feature, you can easily set up custom welcome messages in your server to create a personalized and warm experience for new members. As soon as someone joins your server, they will be greeted with your custom message.

Youtube Notifications

Nebula's youtube notifications module enables you to recieve alerts whenever you or your favourite Youtubers upload a new video. Stay up-to-date with the latest content and never miss a video from your favourite creators.
